Perfect Ulam Idea: Grilled Liempo

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg pork belly
  • 2 cups barbecue sauce (store bought)
  • 1/2 cup Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 3 - 4 tablespoon calamansi juice
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • freshly ground pepper

How to Cook

  1. To give this liempo a power punch of flavor, all you need to do is combine the pork slices in a mixture of barbecue sauce, Knorr Liquid Seasoning, calamansi juice, oil pepper in a bowl. Now, leave this to marinate overnight.
  2. Now, make sure you get your grill nice and hot and cook the liempo until tender.
  3. Enjoy and share a full platter of this Grilled Liempo with Barbecue Sauce Recipe with your whole family. Pair this with your favorite condiments and you'll surely find yourself eating even more than usual it's just so yummy!
