Low Cost Pork Giniling Ulam Recipe


Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 1 pc onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 pc Knorr Pork Cube
  • 1/2 kg ground pork
  • 1 pc carrot, diced
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup pineapple tidbits
  • Knorr Liquid Seasoning, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

How to Cook?

Step 1

Heat oil pan over a medium flame. Sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.

Step 2

Crumble Knorr Pork Cube into the pan and mix until dissolved. Add ground pork and cook until lightly browned.

Step 3

Add carrots, green peas, raisins, and pineapple tidbits. Sauté over high heat until meat and vegetables are cooked and tender.

Step 4

Season with Knorr Liquid Seasoning and ground black pepper to taste. Serve hot.


Simple, quick, and ready to enjoy. Enhance this affordable ulam with tomato sauce for richness or siling labuyo for added heat.
