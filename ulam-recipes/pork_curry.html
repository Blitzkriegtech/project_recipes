Perfect Ulam Idea: Pork Curry

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 piece onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 kg liempo, sliced into pieces
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 piece bell pepper, diced
  • 2 pieces potato, cubed
  • 1 piece carrot, cubed
  • 4-6 pieces okra, sliced
  • 1 cup spinach (optional)
  • 1 piece siling labuyo chopped
  • 2 pieces Knorr pork Cubes

How to Cook

  1. In a pan, over medium heat, throw in the onion and garlic with olive oil. Cook until the onion is translucent and garlic is golden in color. Now, add the liempo next and saute until cooked through.
  2. Pour the water into the mixture, with the curry powder, evaporated milk, bell pepper, potato, carrot, okra, spinach and siling labuyo. Just stir the mixture and let this simmer until the vegetables are fully cooked. There, this dish is done!
  3. Serve Pork Curry to you family and they will surely insist that you make it more often.
