Perfect Ulam Idea: Pork BQQ

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg pork kasim (Pork Butt), sliced into thin strips
  • 2 cups BBQ sauce, store brought
  • 1/2 cup Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 3-4 tablespoons calamansi juice
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • freshly ground pepper

How to Cook

  1. Arrange the pork kasim in a bowl or a heavy-duty food safe plastic container.
  2. In a bowl, whisk all the other ingredients together and pour on top of the pork kasim. Ensure that the pork pieces are well covered by the marinade. Seal the container and leave inside the refrigerator to marinate overnight.
  3. While the meat is marinating, soak your barbecue sticks in water overnight to prepare them for grilling the next day or if you're pressed for time, soak them in water for at least minutes before grilling.
  4. Take your marinated pork kasim out of the refrigerator and proceed to skewer the meat on the barbecue sticks. Set aside.
  5. Prepare your grill and make sure that it's nice and hot before you begin grilling your barbecue.
  6. Place the barbecue skewers on the grill until cooked to your desired doneness.
  7. Serve the Pork BBQ warm with your favorite dipping sauce and steaming hot rice.
