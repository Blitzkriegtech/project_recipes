Perfect Ulam Idea: Pork Humba

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 piece onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 kg liempo, cube
  • 1 piece Knorr pork cube
  • 1/4 cup black beans
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • water as needed
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 pieces chilies
  • 1/2 cup banana blossoms
  • Optional: Serve with steamed Bok-choi or Spinach

How to Cook

  1. Get you pan nice and hot over mediumheat. Pour some oil and saute the onion. garlic until brown and soft, then add the cubed pork.
  2. Add Knorr Pork Cubes and black beans. Suate for a minute.
  3. Pour the vinegar and let this simmer before adding the pineapple juice, water, brown sugar, soy sauce, chilies and banana blossoms.
  4. Cook this covered at low heat until pork is tender and sauce has thickened. Taste and check the seasoning.
  5. With hot rice, you'll find yourself eating more than usual. So, what are you waiting for? Give this recipe a try!
